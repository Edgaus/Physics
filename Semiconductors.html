Crystal Growth

Crytal growth in the field of semiconudcors refers to the process of growing singley crystals of semiconductos materiales with high purity and uniformity. For high structural quality it is essential that a high quality substrate be available. Bulk crystal growth techniques are not yet highly matured for most semiconudctors, so it is difficult to obtain high quality large area substrates. The exeptions are Si, GaAs, InP. Large size substrates ensure low cost device production. First we need to growth bulk crystals which are sliced and polishedand then be allowed to do epitaxial growth of thin semiconductors regions including heterostrucutres.

Why Crystals?

From a theorical point of view, when we are tying to describe materials wich are sets of atoms, the simplest construction or model is a crystal, an arrange that it repeats itself along all spac. So we can semiconductors with a very good carachterization with known parameters through crystals models, but can be improven through the use of doping, impurity made in purpouse in order to boost some properties.
